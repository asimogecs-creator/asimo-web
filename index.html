About ASIMO Technical Club

Government Engineering College Samastipur's Premier Technical Club

ASIMO Technical Club is the flagship technical organization at Government Engineering College Samastipur (GEC Samastipur), also known as GECS. Located in Samastipur, Bihar, our engineering college stands as a beacon of technical excellence and innovation in the region.

As the leading technical club at GEC, we foster technological advancement and engineering innovation among students of Government Engineering College Samastipur. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application in the field of engineering and technology.

ASIMO stands for "Advance Step in MultiTech Orientation," representing our commitment to multidisciplinary technical education. We serve the entire GEC Samastipur community, organizing workshops, seminars, and technical events that enhance the engineering skills of students at our Government Engineering College.
